Pinky Cognitive Spheroid

14 native · 1 renderer · 3 void modes · 1 wrapper

What is this?

Pinky is a cognitive toolkit — a set of specialised analysis tools that each look at problems from a different angle. Some perceive (understand), some act (build), some challenge (break). Together they form a complete thinking system that can be aimed at any problem.

Shodh (Sanskrit: research/purification) is the memory substrate underneath. It stores everything Pinky learns across sessions — decisions, patterns, mistakes. Pinky is the thinking. Shodh is the remembering.

This diagram maps every Pinky tool into a 3D space defined by what kind of thinking it does. The position tells you the cognitive character of each tool. The shape tells you its internal structure. The lines tell you how they connect.

The Three Axes

X — AgencyPerceive ← → Act

Left is understanding. Right is doing. Overlook sits far left — it watches and accumulates awareness. Shipcheck sits far right — it builds and ships code.

Y — PostureBuild ← → Break

Down is constructive. Up is adversarial. Overloop sits low — it iterates improvements. Breakers sits high — it stress-tests and finds failures.

Z — ScopeFocal ↓↑ Omni

Down is narrow/deep (1-2 dimensions). Up is broad (many dimensions). Portll is low — deep security focus. Supernova is high — maximally cross-dimensional. The sphere is slightly squished on Z (oblate, c=0.75) because dimensionality has a natural ceiling.

What the Shapes Mean

Each tool's wireframe reflects its internal structure:

Sphere (Overlook) — uniform awareness, no blind spots by design.

Tetrahedron (Intuit) — 4 faces for 4-6 analytical lenses.

Cube (Overwatch) — structured orchestration of sub-evaluations.

Octahedron (FourEyes) — 4 agents x 2 perspectives, void detection.

Pendulum (Plumb) — hangs from a reference point, measures deviation.

Bicone (Bifocal, Intugest) — two cones meeting at a point. Dual-lens convergence.

Icosahedron (Supernova) — maximum faces = maximum orthogonality.

Ring (Overloop) — the feedback loop. Circles back on itself.

Star (Breakers) — spikes probing in every direction.

Tree (Portll) — branching walk-tree following cascade paths.

Prism (Shipcheck, LayingPipe) — solid, structured output.

Shuriken (Ninja) — flat 4-pointed star. 2D precision, thrown with focus.

Diamond (Surgeon) — elongated blade. Cuts in one direction.

Starburst (Berserker) — chaotic spikes. Explodes in all directions.

Lens (Savant) — double-convex. Focuses to one point.

Dashed outline = not a native tool (void mode or thin wrapper).

What the Lines Mean

Geodesics (coloured gradient curves) — natural workflow paths. The Creative Loop goes from building through synthesis to perception and back. The Eye Equator traces pure perception tools. The Fang Arc connects adversarial tools.

Voronoi boundaries (faint dots on the sphere surface) — territory borders. Each tool "owns" the region of the sphere closest to it. Shows how much cognitive territory each tool covers.

Radial lines (faint lines to centre) — connect each tool to the origin. Longer = more specialised. Shorter = more general-purpose. Tools near the origin are "connective tissue" — they bridge other tools.

Grid ticks — coordinate markers every 0.25 along each axis, like rulers. Labels at 0.5 intervals.

Graph edges (in Graph view) — information flow. Teal = "uses" (composition). Gold = "feeds" (output → input). Purple = "renders" (visualisation). Red dashed = void mode relationship. Grey dashed = thin delegation.

What the Colours Mean

Purple — The Eye. Perception tools. They watch, measure, and understand.

Teal — The Hand. Action tools. They build, iterate, render, and ship.

Red — The Fang. Adversarial tools. They break, test, and find what's wrong.

Gold — The Bridge. Synthesis tools. They sit near the origin and convert perception into action. Load-bearing connective tissue.

Orange — Void modes. Materialised from empty octants — directions Pinky couldn't originally look. Each one admits a limitation in the original design.

Grey — External. Wrappers or macros that delegate to native tools.

Void Modes (Materialized)

The Surgeon
+X +Y −Z — narrow adversarial action
Mutation testing. Surgical exploit validation. Single-dimension stress injection.
Why "Surgeon": Cuts in one dimension to test one hypothesis. The scalpel — precision destruction. The void existed because Pinky reveals rather than destroys. Materializing it admits that some understanding requires incision.
The Berserker
+X +Y +Z — broad adversarial action
Simultaneous chaos across all dimensions. Anti-Supernova: cross-multiply attack surfaces.
Why "Berserker": Goes everywhere at once, breaking broadly. Where Supernova cross-multiplies lenses to understand, Berserker cross-multiplies attack surfaces to destroy. Materializing it admits some understanding only comes from watching things burn.
The Savant
−X −Y −Z — narrow constructive perception
One lens, maximum depth, total commitment to a narrow truth.
Why "Savant": Intuit always reaches for 4-6 lenses — the Savant refuses to. Some truths live at the bottom of one deep well, not at the intersection. Materializing it admits multi-lens is a religion, not a law.
